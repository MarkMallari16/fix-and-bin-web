# 🔐 How to Login to FIX&BIN

## Quick Start Guide

### ✅ **Option 1: Create Demo Account (Fastest)**

1. Click **"Register"** button in the header
2. In the registration modal, click either:
   - **⚡ Customer Demo** - Creates demo customer account
   - **⚡ Worker Demo** - Creates demo worker account
3. Account will be created and you'll be logged in automatically!

**Demo Credentials:**
- **Customer:** `demo@customer.com` / `demo123`
- **Worker:** `demo@worker.com` / `demo123`

---

### ✅ **Option 2: Create Your Own Account**

1. Click **"Register"** button in the header
2. Fill in the form:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
   - Select account type: Customer or Worker
3. Click **"Create Account"**
4. You'll be logged in automatically!

---

### ✅ **Option 3: Login with Existing Account**

1. Click **"Login"** button in the header
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Log In"**

---

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "Invalid login credentials" Error

**This means:** No account exists with those credentials yet.

**Solution:**
1. Click **"Sign up"** at the bottom of the login modal
2. Create a new account
3. Or use the quick demo buttons

---

### Demo Account Already Exists

**This means:** Someone already created the demo account.

**Solution:**
1. Close the registration modal
2. Click **"Login"** instead
3. Click the **"Demo Customer"** or **"Demo Worker"** button
4. You'll be logged in with the existing demo account

---

## 🔒 Security Notes

- Passwords must be at least 6 characters
- Email addresses are automatically confirmed (no email verification needed in demo)
- Sessions persist across page reloads
- Logout anytime from the user menu

---

## 💡 Tips
